Fun Facts

Green sea turtles are what they eat!

Green sea turtles are unique among sea turtles in that they are primarily herbivores, eating mostly seagrasses and algae.
This diet is what gives their cartilage and fat a greenish color (not their shells), which is where their name comes from.
